Main storyline:

Herbert the flying monkey is the byproduct of a nuclear war that destroyed all humans.
At least, most of them.
After the war is over, Herbert is captured by the survivors and put in a cage. He must use every available resource to
escape back to his jungle.

Walkthrough:
    Cage:
        Fly to knock down banana.
        Pick up banana.
        Eat banana.
        Throw banana peel out of cage.
        Catch keys.
        Use keys to unlock door.
        Leave cage.
        Walk out of area.
